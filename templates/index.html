10-K Insights Proof of Concept Project

This proof-of-concept project explores emerging LLM technologies and their potential in analyzing temporal financial data. Public companies in the US file 10-Ks with the SEC every year. These are comprehensive documents that detail the companies' business health. This website leverages LLM technology and sentiment analysis (OpenAI GPT-3.5 Turbo & FinBERT) to provide visualizations and key insights of important sections (Item 1, 1A, 7, 7A) in these 10-K files. Currently, we have insights for the entire history of 10-Ks for Apple and Microsoft. Click on the companies to explore more!
