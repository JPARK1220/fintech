10-K Insights Proof of Concept Project

Public companies in the US file 10-Ks with the SEC every year. These are comprehensive documents that detail the companies' business health. This website leverages LLM technology, in this case OpenAI GPT-3.5 Turbo API, to provide key insights into these 10-K files. Currently, we have insights for the entire history of 10-Ks for Apple and Microsoft. Click on the companies to explore more!
